How To Play

Move: WASD or arrow keys to run your rat through the city streets. On mobile, drag to steer.

Infect: enter a building through its door and walk over all the contamination targets (green circles) inside to infect it. The virus then spreads to every person who visits. Higher-value buildings take more targets to fully contaminate.

Avoid: exterminators patrol the streets and chase you on sight. Dedicated guards circle high-value buildings. Some buildings also have an indoor guard — slow, but lethal in close quarters.

Lady rats 💋: two pink rats roam the city. Run into one to instantly spawn three baby rats. Each lady rat has a 10-second cooldown before she can breed again.

Baby rats: your offspring autonomously infect buildings and distract exterminators — guards will chase and catch them instead of you. Exterminators pause briefly after catching a baby, giving you a window to escape.

Death zones ☠️: three buildings are marked with a glowing skull border. Linger inside the ☠️ circle for one second to die there and multiply your final score — HIGH ×2, MEDIUM ×1.5, LOW ×1.25. A charge arc shows how close you are to triggering it. Or let the clock run out with no bonus.

Score: total estimated number of people your virus reaches. The map is seeded daily — everyone gets the same city layout.
