How To Play

Aim: an arrow oscillates left and right. Click — or press Space — to lock your direction.

Swing: a power bar sweeps back and forth. The gold marker shows the distance to the pin. Click when the bar hits it.

Score: golf rules — lower is better. Your score is the distance in yards from where the ball lands to the pin. Going in the hole scores the full starting distance.

Daily: everyone plays the same holes each day. Share your result at the end to settle it.
