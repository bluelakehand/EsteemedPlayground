How to Play

Launch: Drag anywhere and release to set the first trajectory.

Tether: Left-click after launch tethers to a nearby asteroid. Hold right-click to tether to the nearest planet. Tether time counts 1.5x and the orbit slowly decays.

Dodge: Avoid asteroids crossing the field after launch.

Win: Touch every beacon, then enter the docking ring.
