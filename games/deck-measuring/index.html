How To Play

Reference: you get one good look at a 10 m² deck. Study every inch — that's the only tool you've got.

Guess: use the slider to estimate each deck's area in square metres. Items on deck can help you gauge the size.

Score: total percentage off across all 5 decks — golf rules, lower is better. A perfect 0 means you measure like a professional.

Daily: everyone gets the same 5 decks each day. New wood drops at midnight.

Sharing: spoiler-free emoji grid so you can settle the contest with your friends.
