How to Play

GOAL: Reach the exit using the fewest amount of points.

MOVE: Move your wizard using only the arrow keys. Each move costs 10 points.

PORTALS: Press Blue or Yellow aim, then left-click a wall tile to place. You can only move onto walls with portals.
