How To Play

Watch: a line draws itself on screen. Pay attention — it could go anywhere.

Study: after drawing completes, you get 3 seconds to memorise it before it disappears.

Copy: recreate the line as accurately as you can. Mouse or touch, your choice.

Score: accuracy is measured by how closely your line matches the original. Single-stroke copies score higher.

Roast: the game will tell you exactly how you did. Honestly. Perhaps too honestly.

Daily: everyone gets the same three lines each day. New set at midnight.
